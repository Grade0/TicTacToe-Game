# TicTacToe-Game
An easy command-line Tic-Tac-Toe game written in Python

<b>Example:</b>
              
            GAME START
            
    - | - | -       1 | 2 | 3
    - | - | -       4 | 5 | 6
    - | - | -       7 | 8 | 9
    X's turn.
    Choose a position from 1-9: 5
    
    
    - | - | -       1 | 2 | 3
    - | X | -       4 | 5 | 6
    - | - | -       7 | 8 | 9
    O's turn.
    Choose a position from 1-9: 2
    
    
    - | O | -       1 | 2 | 3
    - | X | -       4 | 5 | 6
    - | - | -       7 | 8 | 9
    X's turn.
    Choose a position from 1-9: 3
    
    .
    .
    .
    
    X | O | X       1 | 2 | 3
    - | X | O       4 | 5 | 6
    O | - | X       7 | 8 | 9
    
    X won.
